About SIRDAL PORTS SUPPLIES

Delivering high-quality machinery parts with efficiency and reliability since 2020 at AMPE in Egypt and expanding across Africa and 2025 at Dubai.

Our Story

Founded in August 2025, we began as a specialized supplier of port machinery parts with a vision to serve growing markets across Africa. Our strategic expansion began with establishing the AMPE partnership in Egypt in 2020, followed by our entry into key West African markets.

Today, through our network spanning Egypt and West Africa, we've developed strong relationships with regional manufacturers and port authorities. This allows us to deliver high-quality machinery parts with the efficiency and reliability that African port operations demand, while maintaining competitive pricing across all markets.
